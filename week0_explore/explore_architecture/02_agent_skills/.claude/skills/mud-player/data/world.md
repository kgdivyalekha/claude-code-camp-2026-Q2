# World Memory

Last updated: 2026-07-16

Two distinct areas have been seen so far: the town of Midgaard (where queen
first logged in) and a separate "Newbie Zone" dungeon (where she was later
found — see player.md's shared-account note). **The route connecting the two
is not yet mapped** — queen arrived in the Newbie Zone between sessions
without a recorded movement command, so the path from Midgaard hasn't
actually been walked and confirmed.

## Midgaard (starting town)

### The Bakery
- Exits: s
- Small bakery. Sweet smell of danish and bread; shelves stocked with bread/danish; a sign on the counter.
- Occupants seen: a cityguard, an oozing green gelatinous blob, the baker (shopkeeper, seemed passive)

### Main Street (Midgaard)
- Exits: n, e, s, w
- Main street through the City of Midgaard. South → Armory entrance (unvisited). North → the Bakery. East → market square (unvisited). West unexplored.

## Newbie Zone (dungeon)

A low-level area explicitly built for first kills — room flavor text
literally says "Kill him! Kill him!" about its starter mob. Good for early
grinding, but likely too small to carry a character all the way to level 7.

### The Beginning Of The Passage
- Exits: e, s
- "A long corridor... you can hear the sound of creatures roaming about, but
  can't tell where." South leads back toward the entrance.
- Seen here: a tiny pile of gold coins (picked up, 10gp), "the newbie monster"
  (aggressive-flavored intro mob, see Monster Tracker)

### The Dirty Hallway
- Exits: e, (s), w — the `(s)` is a closed/special door (room text: "noises
  behind the door to the south"); east also has sounds coming from that
  direction. West leads back toward The Beginning Of The Passage.
- "Continue wandering down the hallway... walls a bit slimy and moldy."
- Seen here: "the newbie monster" (again/still), "someone's little pet dragon"
  (harmless pet — see Monster Tracker)
